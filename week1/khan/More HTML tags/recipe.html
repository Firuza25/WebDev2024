Recipe #1

  • Time: 15 min
  • Serves: 4 people
Ingredients Quantity
Stale bread (Rustic Classico) 8 ounces
Red onion 1 (halved)
Ripe tomatoes (cored) 5
Red wine vinegar 1 tablespoon

Step 1: Soak sliced onions in water.

Step 2: Soften stale bread in water for about 15 minutes.

Step 3: Whisk vinegar, olive oil; toss with tomatoes.

Step 4: Chop cucumber, add to tomatoes with olives, basil, and drained onions. Toss.

Step 5: Squeeze water from bread, toss with remaining vinaigrette. Add to veggies, adjust seasoning. Rest for 1-3 hours, toss occasionally. Serve at room temperature.

Recipe #2

  • Time: 270 minutes
  • Serves: 10 people
Ingredients Quantity
Mascarpone cheese 2 cups
eggs 5
Sugar 5 tablespoons
Hot espresso by Lavz 4 shots
Heavy cream 2 cups

Step 1: Separate egg yolks and whites into two bowls

Step 2: Combine yolks with sugar, add mascarpone until pale. Whip egg whites to stiff peaks, then gently fold into mascarpone mix. Finally, fold in whipped heavy cream.

Step 3: Dunk Ladyfinger cookies in coffee, arrange half in a dish. Cover with half of the mascarpone mixture.

Step 4: Add another layer of soaked cookies, top with remaining mascarpone mixture. Sprinkle cocoa powder on top.

Step 5: Cover with plastic wrap, refrigerate for 4 hours before serving this sweet Italian delight. Buon appetito!

Recipe #3

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6 people
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 1 pound
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Garlic (minced) 3 cloves
Cherry tomatoes (halved) 2 cups
Fresh basil (chopped) 1/2 cup

Step 1: Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

Step 2: In a pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and saut√© until fragrant.

Step 3: Add halved cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook until they soften.

Step 4: Toss the cooked pasta into the pan, mixing it with the tomatoes and garlic.

Step 5: Sprinkle chopped fresh basil on top. Serve and enjoy!
